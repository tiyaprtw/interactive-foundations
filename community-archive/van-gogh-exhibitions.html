From your loving, Vincent.

In the memory of Vincent Van Gogh (1853–1890), Van Gogh Museum Amsterdam will introduce several exhibitions that will be held during Spring 2021 to commemorate Van Gogh and his paintings, letters and poetry.

"Here to Stay."

The collection contains more than just work by Van Gogh, something not everyone knows. These artworks are here to stay.

March 20th - April 3rd

"Step Into Van Gogh's World."

Explore Vincent van Gogh's masterpieces in the museum's permanent exhibition. See his development as an artist in the museum galleries. Get to know his ideas and ambitions and step into Van Gogh's world.

April 5th - April 17th

"Erratic Growth."

Four young Amsterdammers identify each in their own way with Van Gogh’s work and life. They selected paintings and excerpts from letters by Vincent van Gogh, and share how these relate to their own experiences.

April 19th - May 1st

"Your Loving Vincent’: Van Gogh’s Greatest Letters."

Discover Vincent van Gogh’s finest letters in our 2020 Spring exhibition: ‘Your loving Vincent’: Van Gogh’s Greatest Letters.

May 3rd - May 22nd

"In the Picture."

A portrait tells us more than you may initially think. How did Vincent van Gogh, Edvard Munch, Thérèse Schwartze and other artists present themselves in their portraits? Find out more in the exhibition In the Picture.

May 24th - June 5th

"Van Gogh Inspires: Steven Aalders."

Dutch artist Steven Aalders (1959) makes abstract paintings in which he examines the work of major artists from the past. He largely focusses on their ideas about colour. Aalders recognises a similar investigative approach to colour.
